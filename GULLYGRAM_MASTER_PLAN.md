## 3. The Feature Set: Moving from Consumption to Participation

### 3.1 The "Huddle" (Social Activity)
*   **The Problem:** Meetup.com and Playo are lonely ticket counters.
*   **The Solution:** **Lobby-First Interaction**.
    *   Join an activity (Chess, Cricket, Coffee) as an **Alias**.
    *   Chat, vibe-check, and build rapport anonymously.
    *   **The Trigger:** Identity reveal only happens within **100m** of the meeting, verified by GPS.

### 3.2 The "Flash Deal" (Economic Activity)
*   **The Problem:** Small shops have "Dead Hours" with zero customers.
*   **The Solution:** **Impulse Deals**.
    *   A shop owner posts a 60-minute "Flash Event" (e.g., 50% off for the next 10 people).
    *   High-intent radius alert (500m) ensures immediate footfall.

### 3.3 The "Pulse Map" (Visual Density)
*   **The Problem:** Traditional maps feel empty or stalking-heavy.
*   **The Solution:** **Geohash Glows**.
    *   Show "Bubbles of Energy" on the map where activity is peaking. No dots, no names, just "Heat."
